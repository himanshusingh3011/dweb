Samagam Details

Several Naam Simran and Kirtan Samagams are being held every fortnight at different places as per the demand of the sangat in India and abroad. These samagams begin with naam simran on sat evening and end around 10pm on sunday. All the arrangement of langar accomodation is provided. The schedule of the Samagams is as follows:

MORNING SESSION
4 to 5 a.m. SIMRAN (Recitation of 'WAHEGURU' Gurmantar)
5 to 6 a.m. NITNEM (Recitation of Five Bani's)
6 to 11:30 a.m. Asa Di Vaar and Gurbani Kirtan
11:30 to 12 a.m. Gurbani Vichar

EVENING SESSION
4 to 5 p.m. SIMRAN (Recitation of 'WAHEGURU' Gurmantar)
5 to 5.30 p.m. Gurbani Vichar
6 to 10 p.m. 'REHRAAS' Sahib, followed by Gurbani Kirtan

Apart from 2 days samagams, several 2-3 hours kirtan samagams are organised in different cities by the local sangat in that area.
